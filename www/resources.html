Resources to get you started

Here are some online resources that can help get you started once you created your microhost site:
  • Online HTML Editor

    • This online WYSIWYG editor, let's you quickly and easily create webpages without needing intimate knowledge of HTML. More experienced authors can also play with the CSS and JavaScript editors to create rich pages. Once you are ready to go, simply upload the page in the site management console.

  • Imgur - Photo repository

    • Just because you're limited to hosting text and html on microhost doesn't mean you can't have pages rich with images. Imgur allows you to upload photos and images, allowing you to use the direct link in your own pages.

  • Vimeo - Video content sharing

    • Just as with images, you're free to link video from sites such as Vimeo. They offer a free basic plan for limited upload space as well as more permissive premium plans.
